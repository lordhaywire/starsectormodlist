Bar event and dialogue trigger requirements:

Rich Miner (Flavor)
- Open comm link with a Ruster fleet.
- System is not in the populated core.
- Other fleet is smaller than yours.
- You have been identified.
- You have friendly relations with the Rust Belt.

Concourse Firefight (Flavor)
- Market is Outer Yard.
- Outer Yard is owned by the Rust Belt.
- Trigger chance is higher at stability 4 or less.

Grinning man at private table (Orbital Station - Ruster blueprint)
- Market is owned by the Rust Belt.
- Player has a colony or at least 1 million credits.
- Player has a relationship with the market owner no worse than Suspicious.
- Player does not already know how to build a Orbital Station - Ruster.
